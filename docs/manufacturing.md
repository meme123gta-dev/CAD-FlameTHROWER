# Manufacturing Notes

## Process

FDM, 0.4 mm nozzle assumed.

## Recommended Materials

- PETG for general prototype housings
- PLA for visual-only indoor prototypes

## Print Orientation

- **Base:** Floor on the build plate (Z-up as modeled). Minimizes supports for bosses; cable port may need light support depending on slicer.
- **Lid:** Cosmetic top face on the build plate or lip-up depending on surface priority. Validate seam and counterbore quality.

## Wall Strategy

Default walls are 2.4 mm to align with multiple 0.4 mm extrusion widths.

## Inserts

Confirm M3 heat-set insert hole diameter with the insert supplier before production prints.
